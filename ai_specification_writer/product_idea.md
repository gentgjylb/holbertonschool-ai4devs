# Product Idea

## Vision

Enable companies to significantly reduce their carbon footprint and employee transportation costs through intelligent carpooling matching, while fostering workplace community and supporting corporate sustainability goals.

## Users

- **Corporate Employees**: want cost-effective commuting options, reduced travel stress, and opportunities to build relationships with colleagues.
- **HR Managers**: want to demonstrate tangible employee wellness benefits and track participation in sustainability initiatives.

## Key Features
- **Smart Commute Matching**: Algorithm pairs employees by route proximity (2-mile radius), schedule flexibility (±30 min), and personal preferences with real-time compatibility scores.
- **Corporate SSO & System Integration**: Single Sign-On with Active Directory, Okta, and Azure AD; auto-provisioning via Workday, BambooHR, and ADP.
- **Environmental Impact Tracking**: Real-time CO2 savings per trip, customizable sustainability reports, and benchmarking against industry standards.
- **Cost Savings Analytics**: Individual and aggregate savings dashboards, ROI analysis, and integration with expense platforms like Concur and Expensify.
- **Parking Optimization**: Dynamic allocation of reserved spots for carpoolers, real-time availability updates, and utilization analytics.
- **Schedule Coordination**: In-app group messaging, calendar sync (Outlook, Google Calendar), push notifications for changes, and backup commute planning.
