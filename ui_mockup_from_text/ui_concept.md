# UI Concept: iCan

## Purpose
Design an accessible, audio-first interface that lets users discover, play, and manage audiobooks easily across devices. The UI prioritizes inclusion for blind users and people with dyslexia, while also supporting busy users who need quick, efficient listening experiences.

## Users
*   **Blind/Dyslexia Users:** Navigate fully with screen readers, voice commands, and clear audio feedback.
*   **Authors/Creators:** Upload and manage audiobook content, and track performance.

---

## Core Screens
The following screens facilitate listening, discovery, content creation, and administration:

*   **Home/Dashboard:** Personalized recommendations, "continue listening" section, and quick access to recent titles.
*   **Library/Search:** Browse categories, search by title/author/topic, and filter by duration, language, and format.
*   **Book Details:** View synopsis, choose narrator options (human/AI), configure accessibility settings, and start listening.
*   **Player Screen:** Playback controls, speed/pitch/voice settings, bookmarks, and voice-note capture.
*   **Quick Listen Screen:** Summarized audiobook versions with estimated listen time.
*   **Profile & Settings:** Account management, subscription options, offline downloads, accessibility preferences, and device sync.
*   **Creator Portal:** Upload audiobooks, manage metadata, and view royalty/engagement metrics.
*   **Admin Console:** Moderation queue, user management, reports, and platform analytics overview.

---

## Actions
Key user actions supported by the interface:

*   **Authentication & Billing:** Sign up, log in, and manage subscription plans.
*   **Navigation:** Search, filter, and open audiobook details.
*   **Playback Management:** Start, pause, resume, and adjust playback (speed, pitch, voice).
*   **Accessibility Configuration:** Enable settings like screen reader mode, dyslexia font, and high contrast.
*   **Active Listening:** Create and edit bookmarks and voice notes while listening.
*   **Data Sync:** Download titles for offline listening and sync progress across devices.
*   **Consumption:** Launch "Quick Listen" summaries for faster consumption.
*   **Platform Operations:** Creators upload content and monitor analytics; admins moderate and manage users.
